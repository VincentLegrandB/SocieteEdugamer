Who are we?

We take it upon ourselves to offer a unique initiative, combining play and learning
to help young people develop essential abilities through Esports. Our innovative
educational approach transforms passion for gaming into a lever for
personal and social development.






We adopt an interactive and structured approach,
resting upon two pillars :

Appropriate supervision
We provide the necessary materials and a curriculum based on key skills
(frustration management, strategy, communication, etc.).

Personal expression
Each participant can express themselves freely, with flexibility that
fosters autonomy and creativity..

We place great importance in creating a caring environnement,
promoting self-improvement rather than the
pressure of performance.
